Specialized Fleet for Safe, Efficient Molasses Transportation

At Alaknanda Transport, we operate a dedicated fleet of high-capacity tankers designed specifically for the safe and compliant transportation of molasses and other liquid bulk materials across India. Each vehicle in our fleet meets industry regulations and is maintained to the highest standards to ensure reliability, hygiene, and safety during transit.

Our Tanker Fleet Includes:

Mild Steel (MS) Tankers

Perfect for non-corrosive liquid transport such as molasses and syrup-based products.

  • Capacity: 10 KL to 40 KL
  • Usage: Widely used for molasses transport
  • Features: Heavy-duty chassis, anti-leak welding, and pressure-tested containers

Stainless Steel (SS) Tankers

Ideal for carrying food-grade liquids or corrosive substances safely.

  • Capacity: 12 KL to 36 KL
  • Usage: Food-based molasses, edible liquids
  • Features: Corrosion-resistant, smooth internal finish, easy to clean and sanitize

Insulated Tankers

Designed for temperature-sensitive cargo to retain product integrity.

  • Capacity: 20 KL to 30 KL
  • Usage: Transport where temperature maintenance is required
  • Features: Double-layer insulation, climate control options

Multi-Compartment Tankers

Used when transporting multiple grades of liquids in a single trip.

  • Capacity: 20 KL to 40 KL (divided sections)
  • Usage: Multiple molasses qualities or dual-route deliveries
  • Features: Independent compartments, leak-proof separators
